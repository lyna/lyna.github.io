About Hi, I'm Lyna. I'm 20-years-old and majoring in Art with an emphasis in Graphic Design. I currently work at my university as a graphic designer while also freelancing on the side. Although I do not have my degree yet, I've started graphic designing almost eight years ago (since 12); and freelancing for five years. Over the years, my interest in digital technology and art has helped me me gain a few useful skills like photography, videography, photo and video editing, illustration, and even a little web design.

My mission is not only to create visually-pleasing works, but to use graphic design and multimedia for good causes, so if you have a cool project, let's connect.

You can check out my work below. If you like my work and are in need of some graphic design services, feel free to contact me.
